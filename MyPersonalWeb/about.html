ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Sitota Ezra, and I think trying to smile for pictures always feels really awkward.

People will think it looks forced, won’t they? Aren’t they going to laugh at how I try to hide my double chin? But then I remember that quote that always gets attributed to Eleanor Roosevelt (it was actually written by Olin Miller):

"You probably wouldn’t worry about what people think of you if you could know how seldom they do."
If you’re analyzing my awkward face right now, it’s probably only because I pointed it out first. And, if you meet me and hang out with me in real life, you’ll find that I smile and laugh very easily. Especially if you tell a funny joke.

I always try to smile for pictures naturally more often.
